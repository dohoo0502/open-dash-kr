OpenDash Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

OpenDash is an independent motorcycle ride companion app for navigation, ride history, garage, fuel diary, expenses, wallpapers, and compatible Tripper Dash projection. OpenDash is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or supported by Royal Enfield.

1. Information OpenDash uses

OpenDash may use the following information to provide app functionality:

  • Location: Used for routing, navigation, ride tracking, distance, speed, and off-route detection.
  • Wi-Fi and nearby device information: Used to discover, confirm, and connect to a compatible Tripper Dash Wi-Fi network.
  • Dash credentials: The confirmed dash SSID and connection details may be stored locally on your device using encrypted storage where supported.
  • Vehicle and garage data: Vehicle profiles, service details, maintenance intervals, odometer, and reminders.
  • Fuel and expense data: Fuel entries, repair costs, accessories, riding gear, food, stay, transport, and related notes that you enter manually.
  • Ride data: Ride distance, duration, speed, route-related data, and ride history.
  • Wallpaper media: Images, GIFs, or videos selected by you for dash wallpaper features are stored in app-private storage.
  • Notification access: If enabled, OpenDash may read currently playing media information and incoming caller notification information to display cards on the dash during an active session.
  • Call control permission: If enabled, OpenDash may use call-control permission only to answer, reject, or end calls through supported dash controls.

2. Local-only mode

OpenDash is designed to work locally without requiring an account. Navigation, dash connection, wallpapers, vehicle records, garage, fuel diary, expenses, and ride history can be used without signing in.

3. Optional sync

OpenDash may support optional Firebase/Google sign-in and Firestore sync if the app build is configured with a Firebase project and you choose to sign in. If sync is enabled, selected app data may be uploaded to the configured Firebase services to provide backup or cross-device sync. If you do not sign in or sync is not configured, your data remains local to your device.

4. How information is used

OpenDash uses information only for app functionality, including:

  • Providing navigation and ride tracking.
  • Connecting to compatible dash hardware.
  • Showing ride, garage, fuel, and expense history.
  • Displaying optional wallpaper, media, and caller cards on the dash.
  • Improving reliability and diagnosing app issues if diagnostic features are enabled.

5. Sharing of information

OpenDash does not sell user data. OpenDash does not share your personal ride, vehicle, expense, wallpaper, media, or caller information with advertisers.

If optional Firebase/Google services are enabled and you sign in, data needed for account and sync functionality may be processed by those service providers according to their own terms and privacy policies.

6. Data storage and security

OpenDash stores app data locally on your device unless optional sync is enabled. Dash connection details are stored using encrypted storage where supported. You should still protect your device with a secure lock screen and avoid sharing debug logs that may contain personal information.

7. Data deletion

You can delete local OpenDash data by using in-app delete, reset, or forget options where available, or by clearing app storage/uninstalling the app from Android settings.

If optional cloud sync is enabled, you may request deletion of synced account data by contacting the developer.

8. Children

OpenDash is intended for motorcycle riders and is not directed at children.

9. Safety notice

Do not interact with the app while riding. Configure routes, permissions, and dash settings before starting a ride. Always follow local traffic laws and ride safely.

10. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
